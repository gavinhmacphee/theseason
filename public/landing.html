Seasons Fade Fast

You watched every game. You cheered every play. But by next season, the details start to blur. Who had the big moment against the crosstown rivals? What was the final record? When did your kid make that leap?

  • Photos get buried in your camera roll, out of order and unlabeled
  • Stats and standings disappear when the league app resets
  • You meant to write it down, but the season ended before you got around to it
  • By next year, you can barely remember the coach's name

Your Family's Season Journal

Team Season is a simple journal built for sports families. After each game or practice, open the app and write down what happened. Add a photo. That's it.

Over the course of a season, those quick entries become a complete story — the wins, the losses, the funny moments, the breakthroughs. Something your kid will actually want to look back on.

Works for any sport. Soccer, basketball, baseball, hockey, lacrosse, swimming, volleyball — if your kid plays it, Team Season tracks it.

How It Works

01

Set Up Your Season

Pick your sport, add your child's name, and name the season. Takes 30 seconds and you're ready to start journaling.

02

Journal After Each Game

Write a quick entry after each game or practice. What happened? What stood out? Add a photo. Every entry becomes a page in the book.

03

Print the Book

At the end of the season, order a hardcover photo book — a real, printed keepsake shipped to your door.
