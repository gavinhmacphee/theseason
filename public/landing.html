The Little Things Are the Best Things

The championship game is easy to remember. But the stuff that really matters? Your kid's face after their first goal. The team chant. The pizza place you always went to after away games. That's the stuff worth holding on to.

  • The moment your kid made the starting lineup
  • The rainy game where everyone played their hearts out
  • What the coach said that finally clicked
  • The car ride home when your kid couldn't stop smiling

Capture It as It Happens

After each game or practice, take two minutes and write down what happened. Add a photo. That's it.

By the end of the season, those quick entries become a complete story. The wins, the losses, the funny moments, the breakthroughs. Something your whole family will want to look back on.
